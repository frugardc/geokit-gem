geokit-gem by frugardc

Fork of the official Geokit Gem that includes multiple results in the "all" attribute of a yahoo geocode, with the newer yahoo api. Geokit gem provides geocoding and distance/heading calculations. Pair with the geokit-rails plugin for full-fledged location-based app functionality.
